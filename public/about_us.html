About Us

We are a non-governmental organization dedicated to ensuring international students a smooth educational journey. We are present at each step of the way, impacting knowledge and delivering insights to help pave the path to the future you desire.
Story of HIT
OUR VISION

To be the first point of call to every international student seeking knowledge and tutelage on their chosen career path.
To build a vast network of strong, resilient and talented individuals, empowered to excel brilliantly in various sectors and industries worldwide.

OUR MISSION

Harness International Talents (HIT) is a non- profit organization that strives to create a platform for international students to develop skills that will be impactful to the society.

Through training, coaching and other learning opportunities, we seek to build strong, resilient and talented individuals, empowered to excel remarkably in various sectors and industries.
