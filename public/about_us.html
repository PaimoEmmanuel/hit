About Us

We are a non-governmental organization dedicated to ensuring the smoothest possible educational journey for international students. We are present at each step of the way, imparting knowledge and delivering insights to help pave the path to your desired future.
Story of HIT
OUR VISION

To be the first point of contact for all international students seeking knowledge and mentorship on their chosen career path.
To build a vast network of strong, resilient, and talented individuals, empowered to excel brilliantly in various sectors and industries worldwide.

OUR MISSION

Harness International Talents (HIT) is a non- profit organization that strives to create a platform for international students to develop skills that will be impactful to the society.

We seek to build strong, resilient, and talented individuals empowered to excel remarkably in various sectors and industries through training, coaching, and other learning opportunities.

We seek to facilitate great networking platforms for international students to connect with industry experts and take advantage of global opportunities.
